### int( ) and str( ):
int( ): 將<span style="background-color: #ffff00">有字串的數字</span>轉為整數,例如: int('123') = 123,而不是'123',int(12.123) = 12,int(12.123+1) = 13
<br>
str( ): 將<span style="background-color: #ffff00">沒有字串的數字</span>轉為字串,例如: str(123) = '123',而不是123
